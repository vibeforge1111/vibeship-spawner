## Priority Tiers

### Tier 0 - YC Batch Essentials (Before Demo Day)
1. `yc-playbook` - Understand the game you're playing
2. `founder-mode` - Stay in the arena, don't delegate too early
3. `burn-rate-management` - Don't die
4. `stripe-integration` - Get paid from day 1

### Tier 1 - Launch & First Users (Weeks 1-4)
5. `devops` - Ship daily without breaking things
6. `security` - One breach = game over
7. `analytics-architecture` - Measure what matters
8. `ui-design` - Look legit enough to trust
9. `ux-design` - Don't confuse users

### Tier 2 - PMF Hunt (Months 1-3)
10. `copywriting` - Words convert
11. `email-systems` - Onboarding and engagement
12. `customer-success` - Retention = survival
13. `technical-debt-strategy` - Know what debt you're taking
14. `pivot-patterns` - Be ready to change direction fast

### Tier 3 - Growth Mode (Months 3-6)
15. `ai-product` - AI is table stakes now
16. `marketing` - Paid growth
17. `seo` - Free growth at scale
18. `viral-marketing` - $0 CAC growth loops
19. `hypergrowth-ops` - Process that scales

### Tier 4 - Scale & Defend (Months 6-12)
20. `hiring-engineering` - Build the team
21. `enterprise-readiness` - Unlock big deals
22. `incident-management` - Stay up at scale
23. `network-effects` - Build the moat
24. `international-expansion` - Go global

### Tier 5 - Dominance (Year 1+)
Everything else - build as needed based on product direction and market.
